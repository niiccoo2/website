Payday 3


Payday 3 is a (expensive) video game, releasing on September 18, 2023

Payday Games

  • Payday, The Heist
  • Payday 2 (The best one)
  • Payday 3 (The worst one)

    • Why Payday 3 sucks

    Payday is the one of the best franchises in the PC gaming community. However Payday 3 is a let down from Payday 2. Payday 3 doesn't have customizable meelee. It also doesn't have good heist maps and it has a lot less maps.

    However Payday 3 isn't absolute garbage.

      Payday 3 has things like:
  • Better graphics
  • Easier to start as a player
  • More inclusive and welcoming community

    • Here is a guide to two main builds:

      1. Stealth Build

    A stealth build is meant for not getting spotted and heisting without getting caught. It is best to have a single shot weapon (with silencer) and to always bring a pistol with a silencer, as revolvers cant use a silencer. Use minimal armor for maximum stealth.

    A good stealth build would be an A144 with silencer and as a secondary weapon a Stryk 7 with obelisk silencer.

      2. Tank Build

    A tank build would be max armor with a high capacity, high damage weapon. You can also you a shotgun that isnt the reinfield 880. The best shotgun to use is the Mosconi 12 double-barrel. Go with heavy armor, and take off your mask immediately.

    The Real Reason Payday 3 is Bad

    Payday 3 is worse than Payday 2, lets be fair. The reason behind this though, goes deeper than; less maps, worse maps, no customizable meelee, etc. etc. The real reason is the developers, the main person behind Payday 2, who made it so good, was Almir Listo, who isnt working on Payday 3 as much as he did on Payday 2. [Citation Needed]. He was unarguably the best Payday 2 dev. He listened to the community and was a great dev. If people convince him to start working on Payday 3 it will be much better than Payday 2. Aside from other things Simon Viklund and Ulf Andersson leaving Overkill. Simon Viklund was the audio and music designer for Payday 2. Simon also voiced the Payday 2 cloaker, which was unarguably the better model over what we have now. In the design of Payday 3 the model of the cloaker had to be changed. Ulf Andersson voiced Wolf from Payday 2. Simon also voiced Bain from Payday 2, who had to be, *forcibly* removed from the game.

    The characters we have lost (or have been changed) are:

  • Bain
  • Wolf
  • The better cloaker
  • Zapper (Model Change)
  • Dozer (Model Change)












    • But...

    Payday 3 needs time to grow and get proper updates. It was recently created and since payday 2 needed lots of time to grow into the great game we know it as today.
